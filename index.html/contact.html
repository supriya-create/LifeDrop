Frequently Asked Questions

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, it's completely safe. Sterile equipment is used for every donation.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 3 months.

Can I donate if I have a tattoo?

Yes, after 6 months of getting a tattoo.

What is the minimum age to donate?

Donors must be at least 18 years old and weigh more than 50 kg.
