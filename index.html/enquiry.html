❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is blood donation safe?
Yes, it's completely safe and monitored by experts.

Q: How often can I donate?
Every 3 months for men, 4 months for women.

Q: Who can donate blood?
Aged 18–65, healthy, and over 50kg.

Q: Can I donate if I take medication?
Depends on medication—consult our staff.

Q: What to eat before donating?
Eat healthy, hydrate, avoid fatty foods.

Q: How long does it take?
30–60 min total, 8–10 min for donation.
