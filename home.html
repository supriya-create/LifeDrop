About LifeDrop

Our Journey

LifeDrop began with a vision — a vision to create a seamless, tech-enabled blood donation ecosystem across India. What started as a small student initiative has now grown into a nationwide mission supported by thousands of donors and hospitals.

🩸 Reliable Donations

We ensure safe and verified donor matches through digital records and eligibility checks.

⚙️ Tech-Driven Access

With real-time availability dashboards, hospitals and patients find help faster.

🌍 Community First

We empower volunteers and communities to spread awareness and save lives.

“Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood. LifeDrop was created so that no one suffers due to delay or unavailability. Together, we save lives.”
– Supriya, Founder of LifeDrop
